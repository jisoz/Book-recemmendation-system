How Our AI Works

Understanding Ratings
  • User Ratings: Users rate books on a scale (e.g., 1 to 5 stars), indicating their preference.
  • Popularity Metrics: We consider the number of ratings and average ratings to prioritize well-regarded books.
Similarity and Ratings

We use a trained machine learning model to compute the cosine similarity between books based on user ratings. This measures how similar different books are regarding user preferences.

Practical Example

When you input a book title (e.g., "Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets"), our system:

  • Calculates the similarity between this book and others in our database.
  • Recommends books with similar ratings based on user preferences.
Conclusion

This approach allows our system to provide personalized recommendations, even if you haven't rated certain books directly. If we can't find suitable recommendations, it may be due to insufficient ratings or popularity of the input book.
